# Ekembo nyanzae — research note (2026-06-28)

## How it lived
- Vertebrae (flexible lower back, ~13 thoracic/6 lumbar) built for bending and twisting through
  branches, not stiff suspensory swinging — a careful, all-fours canopy mover, not yet a
  climbing specialist like living apes.
- Femur shows strong leverage for pushing off branches — arboreal power, not ground travel.
- Dental microwear is low on pitting/scratching → diet was soft, sugary fruit, not tough or
  hard foods. A forest fruit-forager.
- Strong canine sexual dimorphism (males much bigger/more projecting) → male-male
  display/contest behavior, likely a polygynous social structure, ape-typical.

## Why it diverged / pressures
- Sits before any living ape lineage specialized — this is the generalized starting body that
  gorilla/chimp/orangutan/human lines all later diverged away from.
  - Source: Wikipedia/Grokipedia synthesis of McNulty 2015 taxonomic revision; microwear data.
